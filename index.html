FAQs

Clear your doubts before booking.

What is Sri Mayam?

Sri Mayam is a digital devotional platform for booking authentic pujas, homams, abhishekams, daana, and temple sevas from home.

Will my name and gotra be included?

Yes. For eligible rituals, devotees can submit sankalpa details so priests can include the devotee and family names during the seva.

Can I watch the puja online?

Sri Mayam can share a live or recorded link based on the ritual location, connectivity, and temple guidelines.

Do devotees receive prasadam?

When included in the selected seva, prasadam and spiritual items are packed and shipped to the address provided during booking.
